Keeping Secrets secret

Hello, new initiate and welcome to your new career at Spy.Inc.
Your first mission is simple: encode a message of your choosing into a nigh unbreakable cipher.
As spies, we have to send messages regarding our intel but we have to remain vigilant and careful.
Thankfully, our team of analysts created this nifty tool to scramble messages for us.
Just make sure the person you are sending to has a clue as to what cipher you are using!

Good luck, agent.
